# Logging In to ThetaSecure

Access the ThetaSecure admin console through your browser to begin managing identities, policies, and security settings.

## Accessing the Console

Open your browser and navigate to your organization's ThetaSecure URL:

```
https://nx.thetasecure.com
```

You will be presented with the ThetaSecure sign-in page:

![ThetaSecure Login Page](/img/screenshots/login-page.jpg)

## Sign-In Steps

1. **Enter your Username** \u2014 Type your assigned username in the **Username** field. This is typically your admin account name (e.g., `superadmin`) or email address.

2. **Click Continue** \u2014 After entering your username, click the **Continue** button to proceed to the password step.

3. **Enter your Password** \u2014 Type your password in the **Password** field. Use the eye icon (\ud83d\udc41) to toggle password visibility if needed.

![ThetaSecure Credentials Entry](/img/screenshots/login-credentials.jpg)

4. **Remember Me (Optional)** \u2014 Check the **Remember me** box if you want the console to keep you signed in across browser sessions. This is not recommended on shared or public workstations.

5. **Click Sign In** \u2014 Click the **Sign In** button to authenticate and access the admin console.
